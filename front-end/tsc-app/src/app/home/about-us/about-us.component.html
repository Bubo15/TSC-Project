About us

TSC is a chain of daily restaurants in Bulgaria. The exclusive quality of food and the high standard of service account for the best-in-class brand TSC. The chain also boasts the largest number of restaurants in Bulgaria located in the heart of larger towns and cities, or along the highways and the main roads. Besides TSC brand, our company has created and develops several other brands too.
